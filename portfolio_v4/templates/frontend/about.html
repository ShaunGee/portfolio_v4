Who am I

My name is Shaun, and I am a passionate software developer based in Cairns, located in the Tropical Far North of Queensland, Australia. My journey into software development began in university in early 2013, where I discovered my passion for creating through coding.

Over the last decade, I have been continuously learning and creating. As a practical learner, my projects serve as a platform for me to delve into various technologies and learn their intricacies. I have been involved in a diverse range of projects, including:

  • Mobile Application Development
  • Machine Learning
  • Full Stack Web Development
  • Network Infrastructure on a Corporate Level
  • Open Source Contributions

Currently, I work as an ICT Support Officer at a Large State Wide Organisation, leveraging my skills and knowledge to support and enhance technology systems. I find enjoyment in interacting with customers and finding solutions to make their lives easier. Seeing a customer go from being scared of using technology to fully utilising it fills my bucket. I absolutely love what I do.

This website isn't about selling my skills; it's a space where I reflect on my experiences, projects, and skills. I am deeply passionate about all things "Technology," and my portfolio is a reflection of this passion

Thank you for visiting, and I hope you enjoy exploring my portfolio!
